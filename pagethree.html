FRUITS

Fruits are a vital part of a healthy diet, and they offer many benefits to your health

Fruits are a great source of vitamins, minerals, fiber, and antioxidants. They can help prevent vitamin A and vitamin C deficiencies

Fruit is the sweet, fleshy, edible part of a plant.

It generally contains seeds.

Fruits are usually eaten raw, although some varieties can be cooked.
